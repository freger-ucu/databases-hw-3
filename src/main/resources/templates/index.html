Opposition Faction Secretariat

Three data-manipulation forms corresponding to the three input documents identified in Step 1 of the HW3 report, plus three reports corresponding to the three output documents.

Forms (Step 7)

Form 1: Add a deputy

Register a new deputy into a faction. Drives an INSERT on deputy with foreign-key validation on opposition_faction and electoral_district.

Open Deputy Enrollment Form
Form 2: Renumber a bill

Rename a bill's primary key. Drives an UPDATE on bill.bill_number that cascades to six dependent tables via ON UPDATE CASCADE.

Pick a bill to renumber
Form 3: Terminate a mandate

Remove a deputy. Drives a DELETE on deputy that cascades to authors and votes_on via ON DELETE CASCADE.

Pick a deputy to terminate

Reports (Step 8)

Report 1: Faction Registry Certificate

Single-faction certificate joining opposition_faction to political_party, government, and faction_phone, with a scalar subquery for deputy count.

Generate registry certificate
Report 2: Faction Voting Discipline

Per-deputy aggregation over votes_on joined to deputy, filtered by faction and session-date range.

Generate discipline report
Report 3: Authorship Digest

Per-bill listing filtered by bill_type, with subtype-attribute dispatch via CASE and authors concatenated via GROUP_CONCAT.

Generate authorship digest
