Opposition Faction Secretariat

Three data-manipulation forms corresponding to the three input documents identified in Step 1 of the HW3 report.

Form 1: Add a deputy

Register a new deputy into a faction. Drives an INSERT on deputy with foreign-key validation on opposition_faction and electoral_district.

Open Deputy Enrollment Form
Form 2: Renumber a bill

Rename a bill's primary key. Drives an UPDATE on bill.bill_number that cascades to six dependent tables via ON UPDATE CASCADE.

Pick a bill to renumber
Form 3: Terminate a mandate

Remove a deputy. Drives a DELETE on deputy that cascades to authors and votes_on via ON DELETE CASCADE.

Pick a deputy to terminate
